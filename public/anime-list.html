3. Attack on Titans

Attack on Titan is a popular anime series that follows the story of Eren Yeager, Mikasa Ackerman, and Armin Arlert as they fight against giant humanoid creatures known as Titans that threaten humanity's survival. The series is known for its intense action, complex characters, and dark themes, and has become a cultural phenomenon.

4. Haikyuu

Haikyuu is a popular anime series that follows the journey of Hinata Shoyo, a young volleyball player who dreams of becoming the best in the world. The series is known for its intense sports action, dynamic characters, and emotional moments, and has a large and dedicated fanbase.

5. Solo leveling

Solo Leveling is a popular webtoon series that follows the story of Sung Jin-Woo, a weak hunter who becomes one of the strongest hunters in the world after a mysterious event grants him incredible powers. The series is known for its intense action, compelling story, and dynamic characters, and has a large and dedicated fanbase.
